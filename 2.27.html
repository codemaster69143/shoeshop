Basketball Shoe

Nike Zoom LeBron NXXT Gen

Php8,895.00

The Wolf Grey Metallic Gold iteration of the Nike Zoom LeBron NXXT Gen features an upper of lightweight, breathable grey mesh with a pattern based on the map of Akron, Ohio, the hometown of LeBron James. The lateral side of this shoe sports a white synthetic leather Nike Swoosh with a 3D gold Nike Swoosh overlapping it. A dark-grey leather overlay begins as a heel counter and wraps around to cover the medial side, where it is adorned with a white side-panel Swoosh. The silvery tongue matches the mesh of the upper, while the sock liner is a darker grey. NXXT branding appears on the lighter grey insole.
