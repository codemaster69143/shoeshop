Sneaker Shoe

Nike Dunk Low Premium

Php10,295.00

Derived from the 'Safari Print Pack,' the Nike Women's Dunk Low 'Phantom' brings back the Nike pattern that once debuted on the 1987 Air Safari. An off-white leather upper has subtle grey Safari print accents on the eyestay, Swoosh, and forefoot and heel overlay. These shoes are updated with a metallic silver lace dubrae, a debossed Nike wordmark on the back tab and perforations on the toe box shaped like a sun. A traditional rubber cupsole with white sidewalls and a light grey outsole wrap up the look.
