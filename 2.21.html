The PUMA MB.01 “Foot Locker 50th Anniversary” is a bold and eye-catching design that pays homage to the retailer’s rich history. The shoe features a striking black and white striped pattern across its upper, reminiscent of a referee’s uniform—a nod to Foot Locker’s signature employee attire. The stripes create a dynamic visual effect, giving the sneaker a sense of movement and energy. Accents of gold, symbolizing the 50th anniversary, are tastefully integrated into the design, with “1 of 1” branding on the toe box and the “M.E.L.O.” insignia on the heel. The midsole features a speckled pattern, further enhancing the sneaker’s unique aesthetic.
